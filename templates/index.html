ABOUT THE PROJECT

DentCare AI

DentCare is an AI-powered system developed by AI Spark Squad, under the supervision of Dr. Bilal Khan. It analyzes dental clinical images and X-ray images using advanced AI-based image analysis to detect conditions such as cavities, impacted teeth, fillings, and implants. The system provides clear and accurate results to support dental assessment. The main goal of DentCare is to deliver fast, simple, and reliable dental detection for better understanding of dental conditions.

AI Spark Squad

AI Spark Squad is a student team including Umar Ayoub, Saad Ali Shah, and Muhammad Ismail, working under the supervision of Dr. Bilal Khan. The team works on AI projects to solve real-life problems. Their focus is to build simple and useful systems that are easy for everyone to understand and use.
